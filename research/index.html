About Me

Ph.D researcher at School of Computer Science and Statistics of Trinity College Dublin, and part of the ADAPT research center.

I started my Ph.D. in April 2016 and am expected to graduate by April 2020. My research topic looks at documenting GDPR compliance information related to provenance using semantic web technologies. More information on that is available at the project website. My research blog contain information about other projects. I completed a M.Sc. by research in Computer Science at University College Cork (Ireland) in 2015, and B.Engg in Computers at Pune University (India) in 2013.

Research Interests

  • Semantic Modelling
  • Policy-Based Management
  • Privacy and Ethics
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Context-aware Privacy Reasoning Services
