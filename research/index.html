About Me

Ph.D researcher at School of Computer Science and Statistics of Trinity College Dublin, and part of the ADAPT Research Center.

I started my Ph.D. in April 2016 and am expected to graduate by May 2020. My research topic investigates representation of activities associated with personal data and consent using semantic web for GDPR compliance. More information on that is available at the project website. My research blog contain information about other projects. I completed a M.Sc. by research in Computer Science at University College Cork (Ireland) in 2015, and B.Engg in Computers at Pune University (India) in 2013.

Research Interests

  • Semantic Modelling
  • Policy-Based Management
