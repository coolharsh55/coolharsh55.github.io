milestones and deliverables

lists the milestones and deliverables for RISKY project
by Harshvardhan J. Pandit
consent knowledge-graph privacy risk

Milestone 1

Identification of privacy risks and mitigation measures in literature (Month 6)

Deliverable D1.1: Privacy Risks Management Ontology

Milestone 2

Representation of privacy risks and mitigation measures (Month 12)

Deliverable D2.1: Knowledge-graph of privacy risks and mitigation measures

Milestone 3

Development of research prototype for privacy risk exploration (Month 18)

Deliverable D3.1: Queries for retrieving relevant privacy risks and mitigations based on parameters of given scenario

Milestone 4

Public release of online service for stakeholders (Month 24)

Deliverable D4.1: Online service for exploration of privacy risks and mitigation measures
