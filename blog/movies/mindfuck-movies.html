What is a mindfuck movie?

Sometimes some movies leave you baffled, thinking, consumed, and confused to the point of feeling silly or cursing the movie. These are called mindfuck movies as a very figurative interpretation of movies that fuck with our heads. Some film makers are famous for exclusively making only such films. Case in point – David Lynch, the only way to even describe his movies is that they are Lynchian, made by this guy. Some famous directors of today, like Christopher Nolan and David Fincher are famous because of this very aspect of their films. Their movies are incredibly clever, with a lot of details and subplots being subtle and hidden in plain view. The audience is expected to be smart and think about the film to get these hidden gems. It is like an exemplary book that introduces a new narrative style that leaves the reader baffled, but manages to throughly entertain the reader by its end.

Mindfuck movies are not necessarily movies with sudden twists (like Sixth Sense), or stereotypically being surreal. The basic premise of a mindfuck movie is its narrative, its story and its hidden layers of plot that ebb the viewer on towards being obsessed with the movie’s meaning, the happenings or its conclusion.

 

Selecting mindfuck movies

Like selecting any other type of movie to watch, a mindfuck movie must have at least two of the following-

  • a good review from critics (not necessarily from the audience)
  • a cult following (people are raving about it for the right reasons)
  • a recommendation from a friend (who says its a good mindfuck movie)
