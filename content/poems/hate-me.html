title: hate me published: 2009-02-13 12:00:00 modified: 2016-03-20 13:27:01 tags: poem description: hate me headerimage: ===

hate me, i am not worthy of love,
neither am i peaceloving dove;
hate me, i am not worthy of trust,
betrayal for me is a compulsory must;
hate me, i am not worthy of being a friend,
i am not one of the few who waits till the end;
hate me, cause i cannot be loved or made a friend,
make me your enemy, use me, or call me a fiend…
