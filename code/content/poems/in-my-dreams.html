Lost in the line,
Between dreams and reality...
Outside all of time,
Unchained, and free.

The memory of open eyes,
Unaware of truth and lies;
When I am my soul,
Complete, unadulterated, whole.

Reality becomes the sacrificial lamb,
Fantasies of every kind;
Just another tourist I am,
In the the consciousness of my mind.

Let me be in peace in my dreams,
Where the world has no boundaries and no seams;
Of consciousness, God, and morality I do not care,
The world is a better place to live there.
