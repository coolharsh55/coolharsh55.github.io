The morning light is here,
The wind rustling my hair…
I see the world ahead,
And I want to fly…
From one end to the other,
The entire bright blue sky…
Between those white clouds,
I want to fly…
No chains to bind me,
Nothing to hold me down…
I want to break free,
I want to fly…
To laugh, smile as I please,
To live life at my own ease…
Dance, sing a song or cry,
Oh yes, I want to fly!
To live life to the fullest
No regrets at the end…
Before the final silence,
I want to fly…
It’s time to wake up,
No more dreams to see…
Time to spread the wings
Time to fly…
