poem;
a lucky friend

i sit in the breeze and think about you
how lucky i am to be one amongst the few
i enjoy your friendship like a peacock in the rain
for you are there for me whenever i am in pain
so i say to someone who has taught my heart to spend
dear friend, may our friendship never end…
