Look into your mind

introspecting inner thoughts
published: 2014-04-27T16:00:00 (updated: 2016-03-20T13:27:01)
by Harshvardhan J. Pandit
philosophy poem

look into your mind
dance away into the sun
face up your fears
take a start and then run!
express yourself deeply
do not get scared or be afraid
listen, but do not follow
think, not heed what was said
you are but one independent life
one breath, one heartbeat at a time
its your choice to decide your life
walk curved paths, or a straight line
