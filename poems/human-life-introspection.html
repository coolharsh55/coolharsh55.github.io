instrospecting some cultural assumptions
published: 2014-04-14 11:00:00, updated: 2016-03-20 13:27:01
poem; philosophy;

what colors are all rainbows made of
and why do we see only those seven?
of all those people living on this earth
how can we have only hell and heaven?
