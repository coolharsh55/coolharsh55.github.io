The shallow creek

⌚ 2015-01-19 14:00:00, updated: 2016-03-20 13:27:01
🏷 poem;

A shallow creek of water,
Never known to slaughter;
A paper boat floats in leisure,
Carrying a precious treasure;
A youngling’s future and its key,
His dreams of conquering the sea.
