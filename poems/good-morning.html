good morning

a poem wishing good morning
published: 2009-02-12T12:00:00 (updated: 2016-03-20T13:27:01)
by Harshvardhan J. Pandit
morning routine

the stars have vanished without leaving a trail,
the moon is looking ever so frail,
get out of the bed and face the day ahead,
be firm, be stern, and let he laziness be shed
be fresh and eat some cookies sweet
let you smile as your friends greet
they all ask you to hear the birds chirping,
they all wish you the same – GOOD MORNING
