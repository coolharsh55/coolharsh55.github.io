describing the dawn
⌚ 2014-04-22 11:00:00, updated: 2016-03-20 13:27:01
🏷 poem;

what’s that blue over the horizon
a thin line that breaks the streak
of dark blacks and bright orange
a rising yellow hue ever so weak
the birds that will chirp out aloud
answering with the natures call
the scurrying about of all beings
a very good morning to you all
