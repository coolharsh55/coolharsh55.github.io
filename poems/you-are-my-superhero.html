a thankful epitaph for a friend
published: 2014-03-12 19:00:00, updated: 2016-03-20 13:27:01
poem; personal;

When I’ve fallen down,
Crumbled in a heap…
When that last step,
Took me in too deep…
You’re there for me,
To help me with my pain.
Your hand outstretched,
Asking me to rise again!
In times of need,
When I need a pillar…
You’re the one I see,
Strong as granite.
I’m broken down again,
With nothing inside.
But you never leave me,
Picking up my stride.
You’re the messiah,
The one I look up to.
You’re the angel,
I have put hope into.
I live by knowing that,
You exist in this world…
The reason for my hope,
You’re my superhero!!!
