destiny will decide the end

destiny and the path ahead
published: 2014-03-02 12:00:00 (updated: 2016-03-20 13:27:01) by Harshvardhan J. Pandit
destiny life

At a certain point,
In this cursed journey;
You just stop there,
Ask yourself where to go!
And it doesn’t matter,
What or where you fell,
As long as you’re here,
Destiny will decide the end…
