waiting for someone
⌚ 2008-07-12 12:00:00, updated: 2016-03-20 13:27:01
🏷 poem; emotional;

waiting for her
with each passin day
watchin the calender
where its the end of may

waitin for someone
who’ll come on wings
from seven seas
so far away

waitin for a girl
5’2.5′ tall
looks cute and bubbly
enchanting me and all

waiting for a breeze
i smell the winds of change
she’s like a mint of fresness
i wait in despair

waiting for a storm
thats makes me happy
breaks me to smile
beautifull like the rainbow
in the realms of my mind

waiting for a freind new
who’ll join the lucky few
waitin for a girl new
delicate as the mornin dew
waitin for all this…
and waitin for you
