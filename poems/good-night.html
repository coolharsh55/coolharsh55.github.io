good night

wishing good night
published: 2009-02-15 12:00:00 (updated: 2016-03-20 13:27:01 by Harshvardhan J. Pandit
night routine

of lovely kisses and hugs tight
shall be my dreams of lovely delight.
i’ll dream you all night,
after switching off the light.
your so sweet and special tonight,
i wish i never have a fight.
sleep well, sleep tight,
wishin you a dreamy good night…
