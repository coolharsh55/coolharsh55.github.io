limelight

the limelight of a (new) life
published: 2016-03-29T16:35:00 (updated: 2016-03-29T16:36:34) }
by Harshvardhan J. Pandit
experience life

Staggering first steps into a new world,
The feet small, with toes that curled;
Like puffed cotton, soft to the touch,
Cheeks that flushed as roses much;
Encouraging glances, within eyesight;
The centre of a vivid life in limelight.
