Happy Day

a nice day outside sets the mood right
published: 2014-04-06 15:00:00 (updated: 2016-03-20 13:27:01 by Harshvardhan J. Pandit
emotional poem

the skies were clear
the clouds were high
the breeze was dear
staying at home nigh
so I set on outside
to walk about there
on roads by the side
under the suns glare
I walked with the winds
while having such fun
I sang along for a while
like daffodils in the sun
and so it came back to me
what a pleasant day it was!
I feel happy, even content
I wonder what was the cause!
