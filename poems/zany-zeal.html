channeling all the excitement
published: 2009-07-21 11:00:00, updated: 2016-03-20 13:27:01
poem; emotional;

Of the most anticipated
Things to catch and reel
Is the one still hiddden
The unmatched Zany Zeal

Having the way to look
At life like a tasty meal
Such is the way
For the zany zeal

Problems are nothing but jars
With some unopened seal
I have a way to break them
Through my Zany Zeal

So many new things to do
So much joy to feel
What else i need
Except the Zany Zeal

I’ll stop this for now
Or else mom will kill
And that’ll be the end
The end of Zany Zeal
