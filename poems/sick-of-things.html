sick of things

annoyed at the little things
published: 2009-02-13 12:00:00 (updated: 2016-03-20 13:27:01) by Harshvardhan J. Pandit
emotional poem

everybody enjoys music,
but it makes me sad.
i wish i could do something
and also be glad.
people dont know
what are suffering and pain.
everything for them
is their personal gain.
i wish my friends
would understand though.
but they are a bunch
of a confused few.
life has been on me
a bit cruel
i am waiting to go back
and start a duel…
