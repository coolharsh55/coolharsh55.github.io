distance

on and off things
published: 2014-04-03T11:00:00 (updated: 2016-03-20T13:27:01 by Harshvardhan J. Pandit
emotional personal poem

that distance between us,
filled with silence is strange
those words that we never say,
echo in our hearts and yet
with time it comes back again,
like always a single spark to ignite,
the dormant fire again starts!
