girl

a random poem about a girl
published: 2009-02-13 12:00:00 (updated: 2016-03-20 13:27:01) by Harshvardhan J. Pandit
random social

thats a girl (a girl)
the world without her woudnt unfurl
she’s the necessity for a ectasy
to live full of fantasy
the beauty of looking in a girls eye
staring at hair that curls;
she makes my blissfull heart weep,
like i am crawling onto a hill thats steep
she’s a girl (a girl)
without her my world wont unfurl…
