a poem in a message
⌚ 2009-02-13 12:00:00, updated: 2016-03-20 13:27:01
🏷 poem;

he still gazed upwards at the heavens
of all the stars, he searched just one
weary and tired he still kept finding
apparently the star was into hiding
resembling the great astronomers of past
every iota of his mind was at the task
yet he coudnt find what he looked for
of all the stars, he searched the brightest hue
unkown to him, the star had fallen, and become you…!
