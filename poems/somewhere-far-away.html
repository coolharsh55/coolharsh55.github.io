somewhere far away

romantic musings
published: 2009-02-25T12:00:00 (updated: 2016-03-20T13:27:01) }
by Harshvardhan J. Pandit
emotional poem

somewhere far away,
lies the other side of me,
trapped in a cell, forever,
not to be free…

my soul split in half,
my life destroyed,
dooming everything,
except my love…

so far away from me,
dont know how she would be,
i wonder with each day,
whether she forgot me…

trapped in a cell, forever,
not to be free…
my soul split in half,
my life destroyed,
dooming everything,
except my love…

never did she answer,
any of my calls,
destiny made doubly sure,
i remained too far…

my heart bombed out,
blood sprayed in glory,
painting everything red,
except my love…

killing everything i see,
killing everything, including me
but it woudnt kill the immortal,
would not kill immortal love,
except my love…
