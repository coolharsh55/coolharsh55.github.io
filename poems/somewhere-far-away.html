somewhere far away

harshp.com > poems

published: 2009-02-13 12:00:00, updated: 2016-03-20 13:27:01
poem; emotional;
romantic musings

somewhere far away,
lies the other side of me,
trapped in a cell, forever,
not to be free…

my soul split in half,
my life destroyed,
dooming everything,
except my love…

so far away from me,
dont know how she would be,
i wonder with each day,
whether she forgot me…

trapped in a cell, forever,
not to be free…
my soul split in half,
my life destroyed,
dooming everything,
except my love…

never did she answer,
any of my calls,
destiny made doubly sure,
i remained too far…

my heart bombed out,
blood sprayed in glory,
painting everything red,
except my love…

killing everything i see,
killing everything, including me
but it woudnt kill the immortal,
would not kill immortal love,
except my love…
