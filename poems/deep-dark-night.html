deep dark night

mounting will against the night
published: 2009-02-08T12:00:00 (updated: 2016-03-20T13:27:01) }
by Harshvardhan J. Pandit
night reflection

deep dark is the night
as i gear up for a fight
the time is now just as rife
to get something even with life
i know i may have to strife
even if i used a blade or knife
but at the end shall be light
i am geared up for a fight
deep dark is the night
