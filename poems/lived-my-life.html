lived my life

a fleeting thought about death
published: 2014-02-27T12:00:00 (updated: 2016-03-20T13:27:01)
by Harshvardhan J. Pandit
emotional personal poem

I look forward to the day,
when I shall have no future,
and hope to be content,
for I shall have lived my life!
