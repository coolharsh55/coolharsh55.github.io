time is for eternity

an emotional outburst
published: 2009-02-22T12:00:00 (updated: 2016-03-20T13:27:01)
by Harshvardhan J. Pandit
emotional poem

time is for eternity
but it does not wait for me
i have fooled with it
it makes me pay the fee
i lay dead, in hope and despair
that life may do wonders
and make some repairs
but i know its useless,
that my hardships shall be fruitless
theres no use of me toiling
of banging my head and bloodboiling
because time is for eternity
but it does not wait for me…
