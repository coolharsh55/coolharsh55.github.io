Irish weather

the sheer volatility of weather in Ireland
published: 2015-01-29 14:00:00 (updated: 2016-03-20 13:27:01 by Harshvardhan J. Pandit
poem weather

Two minutes of sunshine,
Five minutes of rain,
Suddenly it starts snowing,
This weather is insane!!!

comments

I was standing by the balcony, looking at the shower drenching the pavement in front of my home. The sun was shining brightly, and I was searching for signs of a rainbow. Suddenly, the rain turned into flakes of snow and the road became littered with patches of white. I stared in amazement in the change of weather. From a warm sunny outlook, to light rain, and then sudden snow.
