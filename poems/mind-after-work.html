coming home after work
published: 2014-02-27 22:00:00 (updated: 2016-03-20 13:27:01 by Harshvardhan J. Pandit
reflection routine

to lounge about lazily,
wipe that sweat off the brow,
finish the busy today,
oh mind, stop and rest now!
